# Drug Discovery & Pharmacogenomics

Tools for drug discovery, drug repurposing, interaction checking, and pharmacogenomics.

- **[DeepPurpose](https://github.com/kexinhuang12345/DeepPurpose)**: Comprehensive deep learning framework with 15+ encoders and 50+ architectures for modeling drug-target interactions and predicting binding affinities.

- **[Ersilia Model Hub](https://github.com/ersilia-os/ersilia)**: Open-source platform providing 150+ pre-trained models for drug discovery with a focus on accessibility for research institutions in the Global South.

- **[PharmCAT](https://github.com/PharmGKB/PharmCAT)**: Pharmacogenomics implementation tool that analyzes VCF files and generates personalized drug recommendations based on genetic variants.

- **[DRKG](https://github.com/gnn4dr/DRKG)**: Drug repurposing knowledge graph using graph neural networks to identify novel therapeutic applications for existing drugs.

- **[drexml](https://github.com/loucerac/drexml)**: Drug repurposing platform leveraging explainable machine learning to identify candidate drugs for disease indications with interpretable predictions.

- **[PyTrial](https://github.com/RyanWangZf/PyTrial)**: AI platform designed to support the entire drug development lifecycle from target identification through clinical trial optimization.

- **[DrugScan](https://github.com/Abhinay-sai-krishna/DrugScan)**: Tool for analyzing drug interactions and optimizing dosage recommendations to support clinical decision-making.
