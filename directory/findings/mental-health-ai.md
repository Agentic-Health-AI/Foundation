## 1. Mental Health Chatbots

### MentalWellnessChatbot
- **URL:** https://github.com/djpapzin/MentalWellnessChatbot
- **Description:** AI chatbot for mental health advice built with Streamlit, LangChain, and Llama 2 (Meta). Retrieves information from PDF documents and offers real-time responses using RAG.
- **License:** MIT
- **Relevance:** Demonstrates local-LLM-based mental health chatbot with RAG architecture. Good reference for privacy-preserving designs.

### InnerVerse
- **URL:** https://github.com/friedcheesee/InnerVerse
- **Description:** Multi-lingual GenAI mental health chatbot leveraging GPT-3.5 Turbo, LangChain, and Pinecone DB for empathetic support, contextually relevant information, and verified mental health resources.
- **Relevance:** Multi-lingual support is relevant for Nordic/Danish context. RAG pipeline for grounded mental health responses.

### Multilingual Mental Health Chatbot
- **URL:** https://github.com/Rogendo/Mental-health-Chatbot
- **Description:** A multilingual bot designed to provide emotional support and assistance to individuals struggling with mental health issues, with language translation integration.
- **Relevance:** Language translation capabilities relevant for multi-language healthcare contexts.

### Mental Health Support Chatbot
- **URL:** https://github.com/Vikranth3140/Mental-Health-Support-Chatbot
- **Description:** Built using Streamlit and OpenAI GPT-3.5-turbo. Provides mental health support through a chat interface with sentiment analysis, mood tracking, and personalized coping strategies.
- **Relevance:** Combines chatbot with mood tracking and sentiment analysis in a single tool.

### ChatPsychiatrist
- **URL:** https://github.com/EmoCareAI/ChatPsychiatrist
- **Description:** AI chatbot transforming counseling with personalized support and expert assistance. Uses LLMs to identify issues and offer tailored treatment recommendations.
- **Relevance:** Focuses on clinical-level psychiatric support rather than general wellness.

### Pandora
- **URL:** https://github.com/avocadopelvis/pandora
- **Description:** Therapeutic AI assistant designed to act as a therapist to provide emotional support for people with anxiety and depression.
- **Relevance:** Specifically targets anxiety and depression, the most common mental health conditions.

### Mental Health Companion
- **URL:** https://github.com/dhrumilp12/Mental-Health-Companion
- **Description:** App providing mental health support through conversational AI, offering personalized advice, mood tracking, and mental health resources based on user input and interaction history.
- **Relevance:** Full-stack application with persistent interaction history and personalization.

### Carl Voice
- **URL:** https://github.com/andrewt3000/carl_voice
- **Description:** A proposal for creating a reflective listening chatbot, inspired by Rogerian (person-centered) therapy approaches.
- **Relevance:** Grounded in established therapeutic methodology (reflective listening).

## 2. Mood Tracking and Analysis Tools

### MoodMo
- **URL:** https://github.com/dnlzrgz/moodmo
- **Description:** Open-source, self-hosted mood tracking application designed for simplicity. Built with privacy in mind, prioritizing speed and user-friendly interface.
- **Relevance:** Self-hosted and privacy-focused, good model for healthcare-grade mood tracking.

### MoodTracker (AI-Powered)
- **URL:** https://github.com/codewithriza/MoodTracker
- **Description:** AI-powered mood tracking app combining three components: real-time face emotion detection, an emotion classifier, and a voice-based mood analyzer.
- **Relevance:** Multi-modal mood detection (face, text, voice) is highly relevant for comprehensive mental health monitoring.

### MOOD (AI Journaling)
- **URL:** https://github.com/yakin-ts/MOOD
- **Description:** AI-driven journaling web app for tracking sentiments and moods over time. Features advanced sentiment analysis and question-based similarity searches.
- **Relevance:** Combines journaling with AI analysis for longitudinal mood insights.

### Tempo
- **URL:** https://github.com/agateblue/tempo
- **Description:** Personal diary, mood tracker, and Kanban board. Free, open source, web-based PWA. Data never leaves the device and works offline.
- **Relevance:** Privacy-first approach with offline support. Good model for GDPR-compliant tools.

### KeepMood
- **URL:** https://github.com/Epse/keepmood
- **Description:** Open source mood tracking app written in Flutter. All data stored locally in SQLite; no data ever shared.
- **Relevance:** Mobile-first, fully local data storage. Flutter codebase enables cross-platform deployment.

### Pixy Mood Tracker
- **URL:** https://github.com/mrzmyr/pixy-mood-tracker-app
- **Description:** Minimalistic app to track mood within one pixel a day. Simple, low-friction approach to daily mood logging.
- **Relevance:** Demonstrates minimal-friction UX for daily mood tracking, useful for compliance.

### MoodTracker (Serverless PWA)
- **URL:** https://github.com/benji6/moodtracker
- **Description:** Serverless and offline-first PWA for tracking emotional landscape, journaling, timing meditations, and gaining self-insights.
- **Relevance:** Serverless architecture keeps costs low; offline-first is good for accessibility.

### Depression Mood Tracker
- **URL:** https://github.com/maxim-eyengue/Depression-Mood-Tracker
- **Description:** Application designed to assess depression in patients by analyzing personal and lifestyle factors.
- **Relevance:** Specifically focused on depression assessment using lifestyle data.

## 3. CBT (Cognitive Behavioral Therapy) Digital Tools

### FreeCBT
- **URL:** https://github.com/erosson/freecbt
- **Description:** GPL-licensed Cognitive Behavioral Therapy app for iOS and Android, built in React Native/Expo. Based on the original Quirk app. Free with zero cost and zero ads.
- **Relevance:** Most mature open-source CBT app. GPL license. Active maintenance. Cross-platform mobile.

### Quirk (Original)
- **URL:** https://github.com/Flaque/quirk
- **Description:** The original GPL-licensed CBT app for iOS and Android. No longer actively maintained but serves as a teaching tool and foundation for FreeCBT.
- **Relevance:** Historical significance as one of the first open-source CBT apps. Codebase still useful for reference.

### ChatCBT (Obsidian Plugin)
- **URL:** https://github.com/clairefro/obsidian-chat-cbt-plugin
- **Description:** AI-powered journaling plugin for Obsidian notes, inspired by CBT. Supports cloud-based AI (OpenAI/Deepseek) or fully local/private (Ollama).
- **Relevance:** Plugin architecture enables integration with existing tools. Local AI option for privacy.

### CBT Therapy Web App
- **URL:** https://github.com/tulikavijay/CBT-therapy
- **Description:** Web application for diagnosis of depression and treatment using CBT therapy. Provides a 14-16 week structured therapy program.
- **Relevance:** Structured multi-week therapy program, mirrors clinical CBT protocols.

### Aishika Bot
- **URL:** https://github.com/paritoshtripathi935/aishikabot
- **Description:** Cognitive Behavioral Therapy chatbot providing easy access to mental health care through conversational interface.
- **Relevance:** Combines chatbot interaction with CBT methodology.

### CBT Planner
- **URL:** https://github.com/imranzaidi/cbt-planner
- **Description:** Cognitive Behavioral Therapy planner for personal organization, life and time management.
- **Relevance:** Focuses on practical planning aspects of CBT rather than therapeutic conversation.

### CBT Resource Collection
- **URL:** https://github.com/cognitivetech/CBT-Cognitive-Behavioral-Therapy
- **Description:** Curated collection of CBT resources, materials, and references for digital therapy tool development.
- **Relevance:** Reference material for building CBT-based digital tools.

## 4. Mental Health Screening Tools

### PHQ Screeners (Official)
- **URL:** https://www.phqscreeners.com
- **Description:** Free, open-access resource providing PHQ-9 (depression), GAD-7 (anxiety), and related screening instruments. Available in nearly 80 languages. No permission required to reproduce or distribute.
- **Relevance:** Gold standard screening instruments. Freely available for integration into digital tools. Multi-language support relevant for Nordic context.

### OpenEMR (with Mental Health Modules)
- **URL:** https://github.com/openemr/openemr
- **Description:** The most popular open-source electronic health records system. Supports mental health workflows including progress notes, screening instruments (PHQ-9, GAD-7), and clinical tracking.
- **Relevance:** Full EHR with mental health capabilities. Large community. Production-ready.

### LibreHealth EHR
- **URL:** https://github.com/LibreHealthIO/lh-ehr
- **Description:** Free and open-source electronic health records and medical practice management application. Fork/evolution of OpenEMR.
- **Relevance:** Alternative open-source EHR platform with mental health support.

### PsyClinik EMR
- **URL:** https://github.com/PsyClinik/EMR
- **Description:** Electronic medical records system adapted for psychiatric and mental health clinical use.
- **Relevance:** Specifically designed for psychiatric practice management.

### Psychiatric EHR
- **URL:** https://github.com/palmergs/ehr
- **Description:** EHR web application designed for psychiatrist offices using the Johns Hopkins style Mental Health Status Exams and Progress Notes.
- **Relevance:** Psychiatric-specific EHR with standardized assessment formats.

### FreeHealth
- **URL:** https://github.com/FreeHealth/freehealth
- **Description:** Free and open-source Electronic Health Record coded in C++/Qt5.
- **Relevance:** Desktop-based open-source EHR option.

## 5. Stress and Anxiety Detection from Wearable Data

### Stress Detection from Wearables
- **URL:** https://github.com/peasypi/Stress-Detection-From-Wearables
- **Description:** Stress detection using wearable sensor data (heart rate, accelerometer) with CNN-based models trained on the WESAD dataset.
- **Relevance:** Direct application of wearable data for mental health monitoring.

### Stress Detection System (fbeierle)
- **URL:** https://github.com/fbeierle/stress-detection
- **Description:** Stress detection system using automatic machine learning for stress detection from wearable sensor data, specifically Empatica E4 wrist bands.
- **Relevance:** AutoML approach makes it adaptable to different wearable data sources.

### Personalized ML for Stress Detection
- **URL:** https://github.com/vickypar/personalized.ml.for.stress.detection
- **Description:** Personalized machine learning models for stress detection using the WESAD dataset with Empatica E4 (heart rate, skin conductance, body temperature, acceleration).
- **Relevance:** Personalization is key for individual mental health monitoring accuracy.

### Hotei
- **URL:** https://github.com/Gar1G/Hotei
- **Description:** Wearable stress detection system that detects stress and provides personalized activity recommendations.
- **Relevance:** Goes beyond detection to provide actionable recommendations.

### Stress Detection from Wearable Devices
- **URL:** https://github.com/kjspring/stress-detection-wearable-devices
- **Description:** ML project using the WESAD multimodal dataset for stress detection from wearable devices.
- **Relevance:** Multi-modal sensor fusion approach for robust stress detection.

### Wearable Stress Classification
- **URL:** https://github.com/chriotte/wearable_stress_classification
- **Description:** Automated ML approach in Python for stress monitoring with fitness tracker data. Uses AutoML for classification.
- **Relevance:** Fitness tracker compatibility makes it accessible to general consumers.

## 7. Crisis Detection and Intervention Tools

### PsyGUARD
- **URL:** https://github.com/qiuhuachuan/psyguard
- **Description:** Automated system for suicide detection and risk assessment in psychological counseling (EMNLP 2024 paper). Includes detailed taxonomy for suicide ideation detection and large-scale PsySUICIDE dataset.
- **Relevance:** State-of-the-art research tool with published paper. Comprehensive risk assessment framework.

### Suicidal Post Detection
- **URL:** https://github.com/mohdahmad242/Suicidal-post-detection
- **Description:** Early detection of suicidal thoughts using text information. Winner of MLOps for Good Hackathon. Uses mlrun for ML pipeline management.
- **Relevance:** Production-ready MLOps approach. Award-winning detection accuracy.

### Suicidal Detection Sentiment Analysis
- **URL:** https://github.com/zeinhasan/Suicidal-Detection-Sentiment-Analysis
- **Description:** Suicidal ideation detection using NLP and ML/deep learning models for sentiment analysis of text content.
- **Relevance:** NLP-based approach applicable to chat and text monitoring.

### Suicidal Text Detection
- **URL:** https://github.com/gohjiayi/suicidal-text-detection
- **Description:** Building suicidal text detection model and mental health chatbot with deep learning models and transformers.
- **Relevance:** Combines detection with chatbot intervention capability.

### Suicide Detection Server
- **URL:** https://github.com/tariqshaban/suicide-detection-server
- **Description:** RESTful API for classification of textual suicidal thoughts with confidence scores, using pre-compiled transformer models.
- **Relevance:** API-first design enables integration into existing mental health platforms.

### SW Detection
- **URL:** https://github.com/shaoxiongji/sw-detection
- **Description:** Suicidal ideation detection in online user content. Research-grade detection system.
- **Relevance:** Focused on online content monitoring for early intervention.

### Suicide Detection Chatbot
- **URL:** https://github.com/Sudhanshu1st/Suicide-Detection-Chatbot-Project
- **Description:** Chatbot built to detect suicidal tendencies and provide help, integrating NLP and ML for identifying distress signs.
- **Relevance:** Combines conversational interface with crisis detection.

## 8. Mental Health Data Analysis Frameworks

### MentaLLaMA
- **URL:** https://github.com/SteveKGYang/MentalLLaMA
- **Description:** First open-source instruction-following LLM for interpretable mental health analysis. Includes IMHI dataset with 105K instruction samples for multi-task, multi-source mental health analysis on social media.
- **Relevance:** Foundation model specifically fine-tuned for mental health. Interpretable predictions are critical for clinical trust.

### Mental-LLM
- **URL:** https://github.com/neuhai/Mental-LLM
- **Description:** Research on leveraging large language models for mental health prediction via online text data. Published research paper.
- **Relevance:** Systematic evaluation of LLMs for mental health prediction tasks.

### MentalBERT
- **URL:** https://huggingface.co/mental/mental-bert-base-uncased
- **Description:** BERT model initialized and trained with mental health-related posts from Reddit. Masked language model for detecting mental disorders in online content.
- **Relevance:** Pre-trained model ready for fine-tuning on specific mental health NLP tasks.

### Empathy in Mental Health Support
- **URL:** https://github.com/behavioral-data/Empathy-Mental-Health
- **Description:** EMNLP 2020 research on empathy in text-based mental health support. Includes 10K annotated (post, response) pairs and RoBERTa-based bi-encoder model for identifying empathy. Analysis of 235K mental health interactions.
- **Relevance:** Foundational research for building empathetic AI systems. Dataset and model publicly available.

### Mental Health Sentiment Analysis (RoBERTa)
- **URL:** https://github.com/indranil143/Mental-Health-Sentiment-Analysis-using-Deep-Learning
- **Description:** Fine-tuned RoBERTa for classifying mental health sentiments into 7 categories: Anxiety, Bipolar, Depression, Normal, Personality Disorder, Stress, and Suicidal.
- **Relevance:** Multi-class mental health classification useful for triage and screening.

### NLP Sentiment Analysis Mental Health
- **URL:** https://github.com/ManuelEspejo/NLP-Sentiment-Analysis-Mental-Health
- **Description:** Binary classification using Dense, CNN, and LSTM models for determining whether text messages are related to mental health.
- **Relevance:** Comparison of multiple ML architectures for mental health text classification.

### Mental Health Datasets Collection
- **URL:** https://github.com/kharrigian/mental-health-datasets
- **Description:** Evolving list of electronic media datasets used to model mental health status. Central knowledge base for NLP and mental health researchers.
- **Relevance:** Essential resource for training and evaluating mental health AI models.

### Depression Datasets NLP
- **URL:** https://github.com/bucuram/depression-datasets-nlp
- **Description:** Collection of datasets for depression detection and modeling from social media data.
- **Relevance:** Focused dataset collection for depression-specific NLP work.

### Sentiment Analysis for Mental Health Forums
- **URL:** https://github.com/OpenGenus/Sentiment-Analysis-For-Mental-Health-Sites-and-Forums
- **Description:** NLTK VADER-based sentiment analysis for understanding emotional dynamics within mental health platforms. Analyzes and visualizes sentiments from support forum content.
- **Relevance:** Applicable to monitoring mental health community platforms.

### Predicting Depression
- **URL:** https://github.com/HeyThatsViv/Predicting-Depression
- **Description:** ML project predicting depression using CDC NHANES health care data. Includes Streamlit dashboard for data exploration.
- **Relevance:** Uses real-world health survey data rather than social media, closer to clinical application.

### FacePsy
- **Description:** Open-source affective mobile sensing system analyzing facial behavior and head gestures for depression detection in naturalistic settings.
- **Relevance:** Non-invasive, passive sensing approach for depression monitoring.

## 9. Nordic/Danish Mental Health Digital Tools

### Minplan App
- **URL:** https://minplan.org (not open-source but noteworthy)
- **Description:** Safety plan app for individuals experiencing severe mental health crises. Installed by over 50,000 people in Denmark and Norway; has launched in Ireland.
- **Relevance:** Danish-developed crisis intervention tool with proven Nordic adoption. Model for open-source alternatives.

### Forhelse / PreParent
- **URL:** https://forhelse.no
- **Description:** Danish-Norwegian collaboration on digital mental health tools. PreParent is an internet-based tool to monitor and prevent postpartum depression, developed with Youwell in Bergen.
- **Relevance:** Direct Nordic collaboration on digital mental health. Postpartum depression focus.

### PreciMENT
- **Description:** Nordic university cooperation "Leveraging Nordic Opportunities to Transform Mental Health Care through Precision Medicine." Promotes precision psychiatry across Nordic countries.
- **Relevance:** Pan-Nordic research initiative for precision mental health care using data-driven approaches.

### Woof (School Well-being)
- **Description:** Denmark-based startup tool that surveys schoolchildren on well-being indicators and uses algorithms to suggest focus areas for classes.
- **Relevance:** Danish tool focused on youth mental health in educational settings.

### Danish Board for Health Apps
- **Description:** On June 27, 2025, the Danish Board for Health Apps released its first officially recommended digital health apps, setting standards for digital mental health tools in Denmark.
- **Relevance:** Regulatory framework for digital health apps in Denmark, relevant for compliance.

## Summary of Landscape

The open-source mental health AI ecosystem spans several maturity levels:

**Production-ready tools:**
- FreeCBT (mobile CBT app)
- OpenEMR (EHR with mental health modules)
- MoodMo, Tempo, KeepMood (mood trackers)
- PHQ Screeners (screening instruments)

**Research-grade models and frameworks:**
- MentaLLaMA / MentalBERT (foundation models)
- PsyGUARD (crisis detection)
- Empathy-Mental-Health (empathy analysis)
- WESAD-based stress detection pipelines

**Prototype/demo applications:**
- Various chatbots (InnerVerse, ChatPsychiatrist, Pandora)
- AI journaling tools (Journal Tree, MOOD)
- Sentiment analysis pipelines

**Gaps identified:**
- Few production-grade open-source tools with Danish/Nordic language support
- Limited open-source tools for clinical workflow integration
- Crisis detection tools are mostly research prototypes, not production systems
- Lack of open-source tools combining wearable data with conversational AI
- GDPR-compliant architectures are under-represented in open-source projects
