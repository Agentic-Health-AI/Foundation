# Meta-Resources

Awesome lists and curated collections covering the health AI landscape.

- **[Awesome-AI-Agents-for-Healthcare](https://github.com/AgenticHealthAI/Awesome-AI-Agents-for-Healthcare)**: Comprehensive collection of AI agents designed for healthcare applications, covering clinical decision support, patient engagement, and operational automation.

- **[Awesome-LLM-Healthcare](https://github.com/mingze-yuan/Awesome-LLM-Healthcare)**: Curated resources tracking the progress and applications of large language models in healthcare and medicine.

- **[awesome-healthcare](https://github.com/kakoni/awesome-healthcare)**: Extensive collection of 3.6k+ open-source healthcare software projects, frameworks, and tools across clinical systems, data management, and patient care.

- **[Awesome-Medical-Large-Language-Models](https://github.com/burglarhobbit/Awesome-Medical-Large-Language-Models)**: Curated papers and resources on medical-specific large language models, benchmarks, and evaluation frameworks.

- **[MedLLMs Practical Guide](https://github.com/AI-in-Health/MedLLMsPracticalGuide)**: Practical guidance on implementing medical large language models, based on Nature Reviews Bioengineering research.

- **[Awesome-Agentic-Clinical-Dialogue](https://github.com/xqz614/Awesome-Agentic-Clinical-Dialogue)**: Focused collection of agentic systems and approaches for clinical dialogue, conversation design, and patient communication.

- **[awesome-quantified-self](https://github.com/woop/awesome-quantified-self)**: Comprehensive ecosystem of tools and resources for personal health tracking, wearables, and self-monitoring technologies.
