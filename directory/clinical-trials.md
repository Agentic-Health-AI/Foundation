# Clinical Trials

Tools for clinical trial matching, analysis, and management.

- **[TrialGPT](https://github.com/ncbi-nlp/TrialGPT)**: State-of-the-art LLM-based clinical trial matching system from NIH/NLM that leverages large language models for intelligent patient-to-trial eligibility assessment.

- **[TrialMatchAI](https://github.com/cbib/TrialMatchAI)**: Genomic-aware patient-trial matching platform featuring explainable AI (XAI) capabilities for precision medicine integration with clinical trial discovery.

- **[MatchMiner](https://github.com/dfci/matchminer)**: Dana-Farber's genomic trial matching engine that matches genomic profiles of patients to relevant clinical trials and precision oncology studies.

- **[Clinical Trial Parser](https://github.com/facebookresearch/Clinical-Trial-Parser)**: Meta's eligibility criteria parser that automatically extracts and structures clinical trial eligibility requirements from unstructured text.

- **[pytrials](https://github.com/jvfe/pytrials)**: Python wrapper for ClinicalTrials.gov enabling programmatic access to the comprehensive clinical trial registry with intuitive query capabilities.

- **[ctrdata](https://github.com/rfhb/ctrdata)**: Comprehensive R-based tool for retrieving and analyzing data from multiple clinical trial registers including EU CTIS and other international registries.

- **[OpenStudyBuilder](https://github.com/NovoNordisk-OpenSource/openstudybuilder-description)**: Novo Nordisk's open-source study lifecycle management tool for designing and managing clinical research studies from conception through execution.

- **[Tri-AL](https://github.com/pouyan9675/Tri-AL)**: Clinical trial visualization and analysis platform enabling interactive exploration and comprehensive analysis of trial characteristics and outcomes.
