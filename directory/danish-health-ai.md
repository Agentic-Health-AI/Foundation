# Danish Health AI

Danish-specific health data infrastructure, standards, and research tools.

- **[Sundhedsdatastyrelsen](https://github.com/Sundhedsdatastyrelsen)**: The Danish Health Data Authority's public repositories providing official health data infrastructure and tools for Danish health system integration.

- **[HL7 DK Core](https://github.com/hl7dk/dk-core)**: Danish FHIR core profiles establishing standardized health data exchange specifications tailored to the Danish healthcare context.

- **[OpenStudyBuilder](https://github.com/NovoNordisk-OpenSource/openstudybuilder-description)**: Novo Nordisk's open-source platform for managing clinical study lifecycle from design through execution and analysis.

- **[OSDC (Open-Source Diabetes Classifier)](https://github.com/steno-aarhus/osdc)**: Machine learning tool for diabetes classification using Danish disease registers and epidemiological data.

- **[DaNLP](https://github.com/alexandrainst/danlp)**: Comprehensive Danish natural language processing library from Alexandra Institute enabling NLP applications in Danish health text analysis.

- **[Health Data Science Sandbox](https://github.com/hds-sandbox)**: Multi-university Danish collaborative environment providing secure infrastructure for health data science research and development.

- **[SSI Bifrost](https://github.com/ssi-dk/bifrost)**: Danish bacterial surveillance platform developed by Statens Serum Institut for tracking and analyzing bacterial infections across the country.

- **[OpenTele](https://opentele.dk/)**: 4S Foundation's open-source telemedicine platform designed for chronic disease monitoring and remote patient management in Danish healthcare settings.
