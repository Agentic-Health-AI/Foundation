# Mental Health

AI tools for mental health support, mood tracking, and crisis detection.

- **[MentaLLaMA](https://github.com/SteveKGYang/MentalLLaMA)**: First open-source LLM designed for interpretable mental health analysis, enabling researchers and clinicians to understand model decisions.

- **[PsyGUARD](https://github.com/qiuhuachuan/psyguard)**: Advanced suicide detection and risk assessment system presented at EMNLP 2024, combining NLP techniques for crisis intervention.

- **[FreeCBT](https://github.com/erosson/freecbt)**: Open-source Cognitive Behavioral Therapy application available for iOS and Android, providing evidence-based CBT tools for self-care.

- **[MoodMo](https://github.com/dnlzrgz/moodmo)**: Self-hosted, privacy-focused mood tracking application that puts user data control first.

- **[ChatCBT](https://github.com/clairefro/obsidian-chat-cbt-plugin)**: Obsidian plugin for CBT journaling that supports local AI options, enabling therapeutic reflection without relying on external services.

- **[MentalWellnessChatbot](https://github.com/djpapzin/MentalWellnessChatbot)**: Local LLM-powered mental health chatbot with RAG capabilities, designed for conversational support while maintaining privacy.

- **[Empathy-Mental-Health](https://github.com/behavioral-data/Empathy-Mental-Health)**: Research tool for detecting and analyzing empathy in mental health support interactions, improving quality of AI-assisted care.
