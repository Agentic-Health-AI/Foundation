# Research Agents

AI agents designed for medical and health research workflows.

- **[LHONOpenClaw](https://github.com/organicoder42/openclawresearch)**: A decentralized swarm of OpenClaw AI agents dedicated to finding funding, mapping clinical trials, and surfacing adjacent therapies for Leber's Hereditary Optic Neuropathy (LHON). Created by Thomas Saaby Noer.

- **[Medical Deep Research](https://github.com/Clinical-Copilot/Medical_Deep_Research)**: Agentic medical investigation framework with Model Context Protocol (MCP) integration for deep research and clinical analysis.

- **[Multi-Agent Medical Assistant](https://github.com/souvikmajumder26/Multi-Agent-Medical-Assistant)**: Multi-agent diagnostic system leveraging specialized agents for comprehensive medical assessment and reasoning.

- **[OpenMedicine](https://github.com/RamosFBC/openmedicine)**: Evidence-based clinical reasoning MCP server enabling AI agents to access structured medical knowledge and diagnostic protocols.

- **[HealthFlow](https://github.com/yhzhu99/HealthFlow)**: Self-evolving AI agent designed for healthcare research that continuously improves through iterative learning and medical data integration.

- **[Copilot4Researcher](https://github.com/Witivio/Copilot4Researcher)**: Multi-source research aggregation agent that synthesizes information across diverse academic and clinical databases.

- **[AI-Agents-for-Medical-Diagnostics](https://github.com/ahmadvh/AI-Agents-for-Medical-Diagnostics)**: Multi-specialist agent framework employing domain-specific models for collaborative diagnostic reasoning.

- **[Claude Scientific Skills](https://github.com/K-Dense-AI/claude-scientific-skills)**: Genomics and molecular biology skills package for Claude agents, enabling specialized analysis of genetic and genomic research.

- **[Scientific Skills](https://github.com/yorkeccak/scientific-skills)**: Multi-database search and aggregation skills for scientific research, integrating information from diverse biological and medical databases.

- **[OpenHealth](https://github.com/OpenHealthForAll/open-health)**: Comprehensive AI health assistant platform with broad applicability across health research and clinical support workflows.
