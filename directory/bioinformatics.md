# Bioinformatics & Genomics

Tools for genomic analysis, variant interpretation, and multi-omics research.

- **[OpenCRAVAT](https://github.com/KarchinLab/open-cravat)**: Comprehensive variant interpretation and annotation tool with 300+ modules for functional analysis. Accepts diverse input formats including 23andMe data for variant effect prediction.

- **[DeepVariant](https://github.com/google/deepvariant)**: Google's deep learning-based variant caller that leverages convolutional neural networks to accurately identify genetic variants from sequencing data with high sensitivity and specificity.

- **[pgsc_calc](https://github.com/PGScatalog/pgsc_calc)**: Nextflow-based polygenic risk score calculator enabling reproducible computation of genetic risk for complex diseases from genome-wide association study catalog.

- **[PharmCAT](https://github.com/PharmGKB/PharmCAT)**: Pharmacogenomics Clinical Annotation Tool that interprets genetic variants to predict drug metabolism and inform personalized medication recommendations based on clinical guidelines.

- **[QIIME 2](https://github.com/qiime2/qiime2)**: Leading bioinformatics platform for microbiome analysis, providing integrated workflows for 16S/18S rRNA and metagenomic sequencing data processing and statistical analysis.

- **[PRScalc](https://github.com/episphere/prs)**: Browser-based polygenic risk score calculator enabling real-time genetic risk assessment without server infrastructure, making PRS analysis accessible for researchers and clinicians.

- **[Nucleotide Transformer](https://github.com/instadeepai/nucleotide-transformer)**: Foundation model for genomics that learns universal representations of DNA sequences for improved variant effect prediction and downstream genomic tasks.

- **[OmicVerse](https://github.com/Starlitnightly/omicverse)**: Comprehensive multi-omics analysis suite integrating transcriptomics, proteomics, and metabolomics workflows for systems biology research and biomarker discovery.
